Dawa Karpo is a cozy and vibrant restaurant established in 2022 in New Delhi. Known for its warm hospitality and delicious Tibetan cuisine, it quickly became a favorite among locals and tourists looking to experience authentic flavors from the Himalayas. The name "Dawa Karpo," meaning "White Moon" in Tibetan, reflects peace, purity, and a deep cultural connection to Tibetan heritage.

The restaurant was founded by a young Tibetan entrepreneur with a passion for food and community. Starting with a small shop, he dreamed of sharing his roots through traditional dishes like momos, thukpa, shabalay, and butter tea. With dedication and hard work, he transformed his humble beginnings into a full-fledged eatery that blends modern style with Tibetan soul.

Dawa Karpo offers not just food but an experience. The decor is inspired by Tibetan art and prayer flags, creating a calm and inviting space. Customers appreciate the fresh ingredients, homemade recipes, and friendly service that make each visit memorable. It serves as a cultural hub where people can connect over warm meals and a shared appreciation for Tibetan traditions.

Today, Dawa Karpo continues to grow, symbolizing the spirit of resilience and entrepreneurship in the Tibetan community. It stands as a proud reminder that even a small dream, when rooted in culture and passion, can flourish in the heart of a bustling city like Delhi.
